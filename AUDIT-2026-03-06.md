# GWDS Store Audit — March 6, 2026

## Executive Summary
The store has functional checkout, products, and legal pages — but there are **critical gaps** that could result in chargebacks, legal liability, or customers receiving broken/missing deliverables. This audit covers everything found.

---

## 🔴 CRITICAL — Must Fix Before Taking Money

### 1. DOWNLOAD FILE MISMATCHES — Customers Will Get 404s
Products reference filenames that don't exist in Supabase Storage:

| Product | Referenced in products.ts | Exists in Supabase Storage? |
|---------|--------------------------|----------------------------|
| Meme Trading Suite | `meme-trading-suite-v1.0.0.zip` | ❌ NO (storage has `meme-trading-template-v1.0.0.zip`) |
| Flash Loan Arbitrage | `flash-loan-arbitrage-v1.1.0.zip` | ❌ NO (storage has v1.0.0 only) |

**Fix:** Either rename the files in storage or update products.ts to match. Then upload the new v1.1.0 flash loan ZIP.

### 2. gwds.studio DOMAIN DOESN'T EXIST
`nslookup gwds.studio` returns **Non-existent domain**. Every legal page references:
- `support@gwds.studio`
- `refunds@gwds.studio`

Customers who try to email for refunds **cannot reach you**. This alone could trigger Stripe chargebacks and disputes.

**Fix:** Either register gwds.studio and set up email, or change all references to `gammawavesdesign@gmail.com` (or whatever email you actually use).

### 3. NO FINANCIAL/TRADING DISCLAIMER
You are selling **trading software that executes real trades with real money**. There is NO:
- "Not financial advice" disclaimer
- "Past performance does not guarantee future results" notice
- Risk of loss warning
- Disclaimer that trading carries risk of losing your entire investment

This is not optional — it's a legal requirement for anything marketed in the trading/investment space. The FTC and SEC take this seriously.

**Claims that need disclaiming:**
- "$184K portfolio from $80K start, 6 autonomous agents, 68% win rate, 2,847 trades" (flagship product)
- "Live stats: 892 trades | 64% win rate | +$7,100 P&L" (VWAP agent)
- "Live stats: 412 trades | 61% win rate | +$3,750 P&L" (Heikin Ashi agent)
- "Live stats: 381 trades | 59% win rate | +$1,980 P&L" (Mean Reversion agent)
- "Live stats: 251 trades | 71% win rate | +$6,370 P&L" (Macro Sentiment agent)
- "This is the same agent running live in our $184K portfolio" (Elliott Wave)
- Flash Loan: "Risk profile: Very low" — this is a financial claim

**Fix:** Add a dedicated `/disclaimer` page AND a persistent footer disclaimer on every page AND inline disclaimers near performance claims.

### 4. TERMS OF SERVICE CONTRADICTS ACTUAL FUNCTIONALITY
Section 5 of the Terms says:
> "Download links are valid for **7 days** from purchase and allow up to **5 downloads** per product."

But we just set ALL downloads to `expires_at = NULL` and `max_downloads = 999999`. The Terms are lying to customers about what they're getting — in a way that's actually WORSE for them on paper.

**Fix:** Update Terms Section 5 to reflect unlimited downloads with no expiry.

### 5. NO TOS AGREEMENT AT CHECKOUT
Customers are never asked to agree to Terms of Service before purchasing. There's no checkbox, no "By purchasing you agree to..." text. This means your Terms may not be legally enforceable.

**Fix:** Add a TOS agreement checkbox or "By completing this purchase, you agree to our Terms of Service and acknowledge our Trading Disclaimer" text to the checkout flow (before Stripe redirect).

### 6. Multi-Strategy Bundle Description Mismatch

The **long description** says: "Darvas, Elliott Wave, **Williams Alligator**, Heikin Ashi, **Renko**, and **VWAP-RSI-MACD**"

### 7. "Lifetime Access" and "All Future Updates" Claims
The Everything Bundle promises "Lifetime access. All future updates included." This is a legally binding promise. If you ever:
- Shut down the business
- Stop updating
- Change the product substantially
- Move to a subscription model

...customers could demand refunds or file complaints.

**Fix:** Consider changing to "Access to all updates released within 12 months of purchase" or similar. Or keep it but understand the commitment.
